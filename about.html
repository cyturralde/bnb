Our home is a 2 minute walk to the neighborhood Harbor Village Yacht Club where you can launch your boat into the intracoastal waterway and in 10 minutes be in the Atlantic Ocean. Boat over to Surf City Beach for a meal, shop the unique stores, go fishing or relax on the beach. (8 mile drive). Or go visit Topsail Beach, a known destination for restaurants, fishing and boating (15 mile drive). As well we have the award winning Wrightsville Beach voted #2 by Southern Living as the "Best Magical Island getaway" to enjoy the day. (18 mile drive). Remember, you can launch a boat at the Yacht Club and be at any of these destinations in minutes! We're just 30 minutes from Jacksonville as well. Want to go shopping ladies while the husband is fishing, we have some wonderful shops at Mayfair, just a 20 minute drive.

Other things to note

Our regions climate means you can enjoy the outdoors most of the year round. Surfers ride the waves at Wrightsville Beach, home of the international "Surfing Surfabout". Board sailing is also popular. Wrightsville's Beach Ocean Association provides organized sailboat racing for yachts, both in cruising or racing class, with the season lasting from April to October. The intracoastal waterway minutes from our home slices off the eastern portion of Southeastern North Carolina and links Cape Fear country with the world. Surf fishing is also popular and fishing piers dot the shores. Boats can be rented for offshore fishing trips or a run to the Gulf Stream. Saltwater fishing is enjoyable in the river and lakes if prefered. There are unlimited golf courses in the area if that's your game. Its also worth mentioning we have the annual Azalea Musical Festival in Wilmington which is very popular. Come and enjoy what the region has to offer.
